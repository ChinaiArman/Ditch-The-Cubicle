About Us

Ditch the Cubicle is designed to get you away from your work.

We strive to awaken your intrerest in exploring through introducing local events. Our goal is to help you achieve work-life balance.


Whether you are a student or a working professional, Ditch the Cubicle (DTC) is designed to help you achieve balance. Work-life balance is critical in maintaining good mental health and avoiding burnout. With there being an endless amount of work, assignments, and tasks in our daily lives, it is important to allocate time to do things outside of our normal routines.

Finding what to do and where to go is the hardest part of planning an outing. Luckily for you, DTC offers a wide range of events and attractions. We make it easy for you to find local events through our event catalogue page. You can bookmark and save events that pique your interest and view more information about the event by visting its event detail page.

DTC is our term project for our COMP 1800 class. We are first year students in our first term of BCIT's CST program. This is our first time creating a webapp from start to finish. We hope DTC helps you achieve a better work-life balance!
